    Scenic spot recommendation

The Zigong Lantern Festival is a folk culture with historical traditions. The scene is grand, the lights are rich, the conception is ingenious, and the production is fine. A lantern, out of a thousand years of history, but also carries the long history of Chinese culture.

The ancient town of Xianshi is not big, the ancestral hall, temple, and wharf are completely preserved, and the stone roads are all stepped on and slippery, and a few streets can not be walked for long, shuttling in every street and alley, feeling the quaint atmosphere of this thousand-year-old town.

Zigong Dinosaur Museum is the only dinosaur museum established on a dinosaur fossil site in China. The hometown of dinosaurs for 500 miles, the mystery of dinosaurs for hundreds of millions of years, and the world-famous Jurassic dinosaur fossil production area is here.

Visiting Deng Ping's former residence, we seem to be able to see Deng Ping, as a communist, in that eventful years when the smoke and artillery roared, "the flag under the mountain is in sight, and the drums and horns on the mountain are heard."

Zigong Fangte Dinosaur Kingdom Park deeply integrates Zigong dinosaur culture with Sichuan ancient Shu culture, and is an immersive interactive theme park that integrates a series of dinosaur stories, explores the dinosaur world, popularizes dinosaur knowledge, and shows ancient Shu civilization.
